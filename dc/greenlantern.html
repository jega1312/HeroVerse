Green Lantern

Real Name: John Stewart

Created by: Dennis O'Neil and Neal Adams

First Appearance: Green Lantern #87 (1971)

Origin

A former Marine and skilled architect, John Stewart
was selected by the Guardians to join the Green Lantern Corps.
Guided by strong will and duty, he defends the galaxy with honor
as one of the Corps’ most trusted members.

Powers & Abilities

  • 💍 Power Ring Constructs (limited only by imagination)
  • ✈️ Flight (via ring)
  • 🛡️ Energy Shields & Force Fields
  • 🧠 Tactical & Strategic Genius
  • 🥋 Military Combat Training
  • 📐 Architectural Precision in Constructs
  • 🌌 Interstellar Travel
