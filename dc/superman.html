Superman

Real Name: Kal-El/Clark Kent

Created by: Jerry Siegel and Joe Shuster

First Appearance: Action Comics #1 (1938)

Origin

Sent to Earth from the dying planet Krypton,
raised in Smallville as Clark Kent. With powers from Earth's sun,
he became Superman — symbol of hope and justice.

Powers & Abilities

  • 💪 Super Strength
  • 🛡️ Invulnerability
  • 🦅 Flight
  • 🔥 Heat Vision
  • ❄️ Freeze Breath
  • 👀 X-Ray Vision
  • ⚡ Super Speed
