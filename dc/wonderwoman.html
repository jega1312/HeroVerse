Wonder Woman

Real Name: Diana Prince

Created by: William Moulton Marston

First Appearance: All Star Comics #8 (1941)

Origin

Born on the island of Themyscira, Diana is an Amazon warrior
blessed by the gods. Leaving her homeland to defend the world,
she became Wonder Woman — champion of truth, peace, and justice.

Powers & Abilities

  • 🗡️ Super Strength & Combat Skills
  • 🛡️ Invulnerability & Accelerated Healing
  • 🕊️ Flight
  • 💛 Lasso of Truth
  • 🛡️ Magical Bracelets (deflect attacks)
  • ⚔️ Skilled with Sword & Shield
  • 🧠 Divine Wisdom & Leadership
