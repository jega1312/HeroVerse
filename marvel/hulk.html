Hulk

Real Name: Dr. Bruce Banner

Created by: Stan Lee and Jack Kirby

First appearance: The Incredible Hulk #1 (1962)

Origin

Bruce Banner was exposed to gamma radiation
during a lab accident while saving a teenager.
The incident transformed him into the Hulk —
a powerful green giant driven by rage whenever Bruce loses control.

Powers & Abilities

  • 💥 Unlimited Super Strength
  • 🧠 Genius-Level Intellect (as Banner)
  • 🛡️ Near Invulnerability
  • 🧬 Regenerative Healing Factor
  • 😡 Power Increases with Rage
