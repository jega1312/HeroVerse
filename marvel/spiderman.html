Spider-Man

Real Name: Peter Parker

Created by: Stan Lee and Steve Ditko

First appearance: Amazing Fantasy #15 (1962)

Origin

Peter Parker was an ordinary high school student
until he was bitten by a radioactive spider.
The bite granted him spider-like powers,
and he vowed to use them for good after the loss of his Uncle Ben.
